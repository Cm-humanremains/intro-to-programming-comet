About Me

Hey there! My name is Chris McCourt. The best time of my life has been right now circa 2019. Being out of work from right before the pandemic hit gave me time to think over some life choices and goals. What I found is that being a stay at home parent, taking care of my two kids was a blessing in disguise. This is because it only furthered my desire to become a better more experienced human being. Being a parent has taught me that it is more about parenting myself then it is the kids. Parent is a catch all phrase. Your're a chef, a doctor, a teacher, a coach. Your're a role model that will fuel their desire to learn and be creative. So, I reflected and applied that same approach. My interest in coding really took off when I had the idea of creating a family vacation app using Appsheets in late 2018. I was using google sheets for tracking data at work and learned about Appsheets. The app was awesome and I felt that if I could figure out how to make this work, anyone can. It used API's to grab local weather information and it used google maps data to do in app searches for things like local breakfast spots and cafes etc. The low code experience was great, but then I thought that I should really challenge myself to build something from ground up. So, I started looking into coding and got hooked. fast-forward and the pandemic hits. I became a stay at home dad. I found a great organization called Code The Dream. I applied for their front end class. I wasn't accepted the first time, but they gave me ideas to kept learning. I applied again (never give up!). I am now a current student of Code The Dream. This website is a showcase of the days, nights and weekends towards completing that challenge of learning how to code for Front-End development.
